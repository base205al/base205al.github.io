BASE is running a development and engineering track at [sloss.tech](https://sloss.tech/) called sloss.tech/dev. ![SlossTech Dev 2025](/images/slosstech/2025/STD20205-15-med.jpeg) We're returning to the McWane Center, and we're excited to bring even more tech content to the conference this year. ![SlossTech Dev 2025](/images/slosstech/2025/STD20205-22-med.jpeg#floatright)

At last year's [sloss.tech/dev](slosstechdev2025), we had a fantastic time running the track and engaging with the community. We will definitely have an exciting lineup of speakers and sessions planned (updates here as they come in), covering a wide range of topics in development and engineering.![SlossTech Dev 2025](/images/slosstech/2025/STD20205-28-med.jpeg#floatright)

# Call for Proposals

Birmingham Area Software Enthusiasts and sloss.tech have officially opened submissions for the Developer Track at SlossTech - Round 2! We’re looking for builders, engineers, and technical leaders who want to share real work and ideas with the community.

If you’ve got experience in:

- Software engineering
- DevOps / cloud / infrastructure
- AI / ML
- Security
- Frontend / UX engineering
- Data / platforms
- Real-world lessons learned (the wins and the failures)

 then we’d love to hear from you. We’re looking for talks that are technical, practical, and actionable. We want to hear about the real work you’ve done, the challenges you’ve faced, and the lessons you’ve learned along the way.

Submit your proposal [here (Sessionize Link)](https://sessionize.com/sloss-tech-2026/).  The deadline for submissions is March 23, 2026.

We can’t wait to see what you have to share with the community! Help us showcase the talent shaping Birmingham’s tech ecosystem and beyond.
